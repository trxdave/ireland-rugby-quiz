Ireland Rugby Quiz

Instruction on How to play!


  • 1. Welcome participants and explain the rules of the quiz.

  • 2. Questions related to Ireland rugby. You will be asked a question, must try to guess which came first.

  • 3. You will have 1 points each correct answer

  • 4. After each question, collect the answers

  • 5. The aim of the question is gain as many points correctly answering the most questions.
